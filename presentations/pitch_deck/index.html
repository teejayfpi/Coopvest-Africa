01

The Problem

Manual Record Keeping

Millions of salaried workers rely on cooperative savings, yet most systems are still managed with paper records and spreadsheets.

Poor Transparency

Members struggle to track their savings, leading to distrust and confusion about their actual balances.

Inefficient Processes

Loan applications take excessive time to process, and administrators struggle with effective record management.

These challenges affect millions of workers who depend on cooperative societies for financial inclusion.
